The **Aloft data portal** is a community-driven initiative to provide access to biological data derived from European weather radars. For more information on the data, see the [FAQ](/faq/).

## Who's behind this?

This portal is maintained by members of the radar aeroecology community, in particular:

- [Peter Desmet](https://orcid.org/0000-0002-8442-8025) (INBO)
- [Cecilia Nilsson](https://orcid.org/0000-0001-8957-4411) (Lund University)
- [Günther Haase](https://orcid.org/0000-0001-7753-6055) (SMHI)
- [Bart Kranstauber](https://orcid.org/0000-0001-8303-780X) (UvA)

The work is supported through several research projects, including [GloBAM](https://globam.science) and [HiRAD](https://hirad.science).

## How can you contribute?

Have a suggestion or noticed a typo or bug? Submit an issue on [GitHub](https://github.com/aloftdata/aloftdata.eu/issues).

Want to keep up with all things radar aeroecology? Join the [mailing list](https://groups.google.com/g/radar-aeroecology).
